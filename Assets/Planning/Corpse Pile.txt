Corpse Pile - 
Fill your corpse pile to increase light, 
with the vampires that come running at you from the darkness.

Visuals: 
2D Top-down Pixel Art
Simplistic color palette / black, green..?

Mechanics:
Vanquish vampires with an arsenal of weapons
Carry vampires to pile to increase light
Vampires enter from the darkness, burst into flames and chase you down
The less light you have, the closer the vampires are.
